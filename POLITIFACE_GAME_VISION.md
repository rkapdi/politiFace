# Politiface — Game Vision & North Star

*Reference doc, captured 2026-07-02. **Status: DEFERRED.** This is the long-term "Politiface as a game" vision. Near-term execution is the **educational tool / FCLE / institutional-license** direction (strongest current signal — see foot of doc), which funds this. Do not lose this; it's the destination.*

---

## Why — the mission
A lifelong project to promote **political literacy worldwide** by helping people **recognize power** and feel able to act. The product we ultimately sell is **agency** — the feeling (earned, real) of taking power back from an opaque system. Noble *and* lucrative: in a polarized, low-trust, doomscrolling world, the scarcest emotion is *"I understand this and I have some power here."* The addressable audience isn't students — it's every politically-anxious adult on earth.

## What we're really building
Not a civics app — **the global, nonpartisan trust layer for understanding power.** Civics content and politician faces are the *vehicle*, not the point. The durable asset is a structured, sourced, nonpartisan **"Power Graph"** (the IMDb of power: every official, office, institution, law, landmark action, succession line, relationship) that compounds, becomes infrastructure others depend on, and can't be credibly replicated without the same neutrality. The game is distribution; the Power Graph is the company.

## The agency thesis — how a user acquires agency (the 8 rungs)
Agency = **competence** (real understanding) + **efficacy belief** (internal political efficacy) + **a bridge to action**. Its opposite isn't ignorance — it's **helplessness/illegibility**. The doomscroll floods you with *information* and destroys *orientation*. Politiface's job: convert an illegible system into a legible map you can locate yourself within and act on. The rungs:
1. **Faces** → de-anonymize power (recognition = first transfer of power to the citizen).
2. **Roles/structure** → the org chart of power; diffuse rage becomes targeted, informed pressure.
3. **IMDb-of-power / career timelines** → learn the *grammar* of power (transferable meta-skill).
4. **FSRS spaced repetition** → transient outrage becomes durable, owned competence (identity substrate).
5. **Active recall (the game)** → mastery experiences (Bandura self-efficacy engine).
6. **Benchmarks / leaderboards** → relative competence + belonging.
7. **Live/collective play** → collective efficacy.
8. **Real-life action bridge** → felt agency becomes *real* agency. *(Framed: rungs 1–7 make you a perfect **receiver** decoding the world; rung 8 makes you a **transmitter** injecting signal back. Receiver → transmitter.)* Rung 8 keeps the thesis honest (not a con). **Detailed rung-8 design TBD.**

Even pre-action, felt agency is real value — we relieve political *helplessness* with genuine, retained competence (Calm sells fading calm; we sell compounding capability).

## The grand play (strategy)
- **Moat = the Power Graph** (compounding, infrastructure-grade, trusted because nonpartisan + cited).
- **Wedge = faces**, the universal kernel: language-independent, pre-literate, viral, emotionally sticky, and present in *every* country. The engine is globally replicable; only content localizes.
- **Flywheel:** free face-game (daily habit + live-class virality + streaks) → mass users → B2C "Civic Agency" subscription + institutional licenses → revenue + **grants** fund more content/countries → wider graph → more users/funders → repeat. Philanthropy pays for content the market can't yet justify (breaks the cold-start).
- **Expansion waves:** (1) US + Florida beachhead → (2) reposition & scale US, turn on grants → (3) English-speaking democracies (UK/Canada/Australia/India) → (4) global default civic-literacy layer.
- **Five revenue engines:** institutional licensing · B2C "Civic Agency" subscription · philanthropy/grants · **Power Graph as data/API** (Bloomberg-terminal-of-power, the sleeper) · civic partnerships/sponsorship.
- **Two boldest bets:** (a) **dual-entity structure** — nonprofit foundation (mission, content, grants, trust) + for-profit (product, scale) — solves grant eligibility, trust, and venture scale at once; (b) **AI as content-production engine** to build the global graph fast (turns a 10-yr editorial slog into ~2).
- **Recurring growth trigger:** every election, everywhere, is a free global demand surge — own the "understand your ballot / know these faces" moment.

## The game-design pillars
1. **North star = the anti-doomscroll.** We compete with TikTok/news for the same minutes and neurochemistry, but *metabolize attention into agency, not anxiety.* Every session ends more oriented and capable. Unifying image: **"a game about turning on the lights"** — power lives in shadow; you illuminate it.
2. **Information theory is the engine.** Surprise = bits = dopamine (reward-prediction-error) → hold every user near an **~85% success rate** (flow channel / optimal learning); extend FSRS into an uncertainty-targeting difficulty engine. Comprehension = **compression**; the core reward is the **"compression click"** (chaos snapping into a compact schema). The minted resource is **uncertainty reduction about the player's own world.** Be a high-signal, low-noise channel (opposite of the feed).
3. **Humane dopamine loops.** Micro (seconds: recognize → juicy feedback), session (minutes: "briefing" → map lights up → benchmark → deliberate *end* → curiosity-gap hook), meta (weeks: identity/map/streak). **Vary the upside (delight/discovery/rare reveals), never the downside** — no loss-anxiety, no loot boxes, no hearts-panic. Investment via IKEA effect: you're always *building your civic self.*
4. **Sensory language.** **Escape the red/blue binary entirely** (tribal triggers that would break the nonpartisan moat). Palette: deep **ink/navy-charcoal** ground (un-illuminated power) + signature **gold/amber** (insight/illumination/earned knowledge) + **cyan/teal** (clarity/signal). States: mastery = gold glow; review = dim/unlit; wrong = soft-amber **"not yet,"** never punishing red (shame suppresses learning). **Color is a data channel** — read your competence at a glance. **Words ban the vocabulary of school** (no quiz/test/score/wrong) → *intel, briefing, decode, recognize, expose, you cracked it.* Voice: knowing, slightly insurgent ally — anti-establishment in spirit, rigorously nonpartisan in content. Sound/haptics as dopamine delivery (signature recognition chime, "compression click," ascending mastery tones).
5. **Progression = identity manufacture.** Ladder is the rungs made into status tiers: **Bystander → Observer → Citizen → Power-Reader → …** (you *become* something, not earn a number). Central artifact = **your Power Map**: a dark constellation of power you *illuminate*; faces begin as **silhouettes** and resolve into full color as you master them (recognition = illumination, literally). Living government means the map genuinely decays → review reframed as "keep your map lit, power moved while you were away." Streaks as identity/pride (buffered), not anxiety.
6. **Content engineered for the click:** chunking (schemas not lists), character & narrative (faces as characters with arcs — IMDb energy), curiosity gaps (Loewenstein — open then close), and rewarding **connections** (comprehension = seeing edges in the graph).
7. **Collective layer.** Synchronous live game as high-arousal communal spectacle (projector leaderboard, dramatic reveals, comebacks) = virality + faculty wedge. Cohort leagues, "you vs. the nation," shareable nonpartisan status badges. **Guardrail: compete on knowledge of the system, never on opinions** (keeps it off the partisan battlefield).
8. **Designing toward rung 8.** Real actions are the rarest, highest-dopamine "quests"; the **living layer turns real politics into dynamic missions** (election nearing, bill moving, your recognized rep acting). Actions are **pre-decoded, one-tap, fully understood** — we hand the map and levers, the player chooses direction (**informed action, never steered** — the nonpartisan guardrail). Dopamine of rung 8 = *"I did a real thing, it was legible, it was mine."*

**Ethics = the moat, not a footnote.** Using persuasion/dopamine tech on a *political* product is uniquely dangerous. Humane loops (reward real competence/action only), nonpartisan by construction (no red/blue, compete on knowledge, hand levers without steering), transparent and on the player's side (sessions *end*; you leave energized, not depleted). The reason a low-trust population trusts us with their political brain is that we visibly refuse to manipulate them. **Ethical design is the differentiator the growth engine runs on.**

**Felt signature:** *Open it for 90 seconds like the scroll. Faces rise out of the dark and resolve under your eye. Something clicks — you can see who runs the thing you were furious about last night. Your map glows brighter, the country's average sits below you, tomorrow's hook is set. You put the phone down not anxious about power — but like you've got your hands on it.*

# Pivot Playbook

*Living doc. Companion to `POLITIFACE_GAME_VISION.md`. That doc is the **destination** (the
game-based, global "recognize power / feel agency" product built on the Power Graph). This
doc is the **route**: when we pivot, what triggers it, and — most importantly — the moves we
must start DURING the FCLE phase because they have long lead times.*

*Status: kickoff scaffold. To be worked through in a dedicated strategy session, open prose,
one thread at a time. Answers get filled in here so continuity survives across sessions.*

---

## Why this exists now, not later

The current trajectory (FCLE prep + Florida institutional licensing) has a hard ceiling around
**$1M ARR at total victory** (per the Monte Carlo ARR model, 2026-07). The game/global vision is
the path *past* that ceiling. The pivot is deliberate and FCLE-funded, not a rescue. We plan it
now because **the highest-leverage pivot moves cannot be made at pivot time** — they have lead
times measured in quarters. Planning early is the whole point.

## The two clocks

**Clock A — start now (long lead time; cannot wait for the trigger):**

1. **Dual-entity structure** (nonprofit foundation + for-profit). Unlocks grant eligibility,
   the trust posture, and venture scale at once. Legal/tax setup is months. *Question: when do
   we scope this with counsel, and what's the minimum viable version?*
2. **Grant groundwork.** Philanthropy funds the content long-pole that the market can't yet
   justify (breaks the cold-start). Civic-ed funder cycles run 6–18 months. `V2_MONETIZATION.md`
   flags an unresolved question: *are grants available to a for-profit at all?* That answer gates
   the entity structure above and is a near-term research task, not a pivot-time one.
3. **No-regret architecture and brand.** Build FCLE *as* early Power Graph so none of it is
   throwaway: the entity/office/law/case graph schema, the faces kernel, FSRS positioned to
   become an uncertainty-targeting engine, and the brand choices (escape red/blue, the
   illumination aesthetic, the vocabulary ban on "quiz/test/score"). *Question: which concrete
   choices in the next few months would be expensive to retrofit, and which do we lock now?*
4. **Ethics/governance framework.** Persuasion and dopamine tech on a political product is
   uniquely dangerous; the ethics ARE the moat. We need the humane-loop guardrails written down
   before we build the loops, not after.

**Clock B — trigger-gated (decide the WHEN, can wait):**

5. **The pivot trigger.** What metric or event flips us from "harvest FCLE" to "invest in the
   game/global product"? Candidates: a revenue floor, a retention proof, the first institutional
   deal closing, a grant secured, a user-base threshold. Pick the signal now; act on it later.
6. **Repositioning sequencing.** Moving the narrative from "FCLE exam prep" to "global civic
   agency" without alienating the faculty/institutional base that funds the pivot.
7. **The game MVP.** Which of the eight game-design pillars are v1 of "the game" (the
   anti-doomscroll face loop, humane dopamine, the illumination/Power-Map aesthetic) versus later
   (rung-8 action bridge, the collective layer, global)?
8. **Global expansion mechanics.** The faces kernel is language-independent and globally
   replicable; the AI content engine is what makes a 10-year editorial slog a ~2-year one. First
   non-US market, and the content pipeline to get there.
9. **The sleeper — Power Graph as data/API.** The "Bloomberg terminal of power" as a distinct
   revenue engine. Whether and when it becomes real.

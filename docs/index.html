Built to make it stick

Not flashcards. A memory engine.

Politiface combines a proven spaced-repetition algorithm with a game that bets on what you actually know.

R

Spaced-repetition memory

Powered by FSRS — the algorithm behind Anki. Politiface predicts when you're about to forget a face and surfaces it just before you do. Every review tightens the schedule.

%

Confidence-scored trivia

Lock in how sure you are — Guess, Pretty Sure, or 100%. Get rewarded for being calibrated and punished for bluffing. End each run with one of four archetypes.

A

The Atlas

Every Cabinet secretary, Supreme Court Justice, and congressional leader — browsable, searchable, and grouped by branch. A living map of who holds power.

✓

Private by design

No sign-up, no email, no political profiling. Your streak, progress, and review history live in a local database on your phone. We collect no personal data.
